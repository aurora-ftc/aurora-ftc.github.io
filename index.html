About Us

We are Aurora 23295, a FIRST Tech Challenge Team located in Medford, Oregon. Our team started in 2023 at St. Mary’s School of Medford, Oregon as part of the SOAR League. Our team is very diverse, with members coming from three different countries (Korea, China, US). This is our third year as an FTC team. Last year, we were honored to receive the Connect award and advanced to the playoffs of the Oregon State Championship. With a new robotics season comes a new drive to compete and improve further. This is why our team values commitment, passion, and cooperation. As such, our team culture encourages every member to contribute to our outreach, robot development process, and team media_comp. This ensures that everyone gets a chance to participate in all three crucial aspects of robotics and improve individually.
